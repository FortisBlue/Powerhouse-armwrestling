Our Story

Become a Powerhouse

Founded on the Gold Coast in 2024 by a crew of serious pullers who wanted a real home for the sport. Not a sideshow — a dedicated club where competitors and beginners train side by side.

We train the full technical game: back pressure, pronation, top-roll, hook, wrist strength and explosive speed. $30/month, no lock-in.
