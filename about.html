Our Story

Built on the Gold Coast.
Built to Last.

Powerhouse Armwrestling Club was founded in April 2020 by a group of Gold Coast athletes who wanted a real home for the sport — not a side show at a gym, but a dedicated club where serious competitors and curious beginners are both welcome. We're volunteer-run, not-for-profit, and every dollar goes back into the club.

The Club

Who We Are

We're an incorporated association — which means we're structured, accountable, and built to last. No one is paying themselves to run this club. It's coaches, members, and committee putting in the hours because they love the sport.

From complete beginners to athletes chasing national titles, everyone trains together. That's what makes the sessions work — you learn fast when you're pulling against people better than you, and you get stronger when the newcomers push you to explain what you're doing.

We cover the full technical game every session: back pressure, pronation, the top-roll, the hook, wrist curl strength, and explosive pulling speed. Show up enough times and you'll surprise yourself.

The Long Game

The Powerhouse Pillars

From day one, the plan has been to build from the foundations up. We call these the Powerhouse Pillars — the four things that turn a good club into a great one.

01
Strong Membership Base
A thriving, structured membership that funds the club, grows the community, and keeps training accessible for everyone.
02
Elite Athlete Development
Building pathways to state, national, and World Armwrestling Federation (WAF) competition. Gold Coast on the world stage.
03
Dedicated Training Facility
A permanent home with professional competition tables — permanently set up, always ready. No more packing in and out.
04
Queensland's Benchmark Club
Setting the standard for how arm wrestling clubs operate — structured, technical, inclusive, and respected at every level of the sport.
