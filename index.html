Our Story

Become a Powerhouse

Powerhouse Armwrestling was founded in April 2020 by a group of Gold Coast athletes who wanted a real home for the sport — not a side show at a gym, but a dedicated club where serious competitors and curious beginners are both welcome.

We train the full technical game: back pressure, pronation, the top-roll, the hook, wrist curl strength and explosive speed. Whether you've never sat at a table or you're gunning for nationals, our coaches meet you where you are.

Structured technical coaching every session
Competition table training (pegs & straps)
Strength programming for arm wrestling
Regular in-house ladders & ranked events
Pathways to state & national competition
All ages, genders & experience levels welcome

Membership is $192/year — no lock-in contract, no inflated gym fees. Show up, train hard, and become part of one of Australia's fastest-growing arm wrestling clubs.
